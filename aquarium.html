THE AQUARIUM

The world's most magical aquarium.

Pine City Zoo's Aquarium houses over hundred different types of species and animals.When you walk in, it creates the feeling of an open ocean, and huge glasses are located in a semicircle around you, which gives even greater realism to the feeling that you are in the ocean among fish.

Bring your family and kids in to have an oceanic experience.


WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT:

You can find the Aquarium at arena D01, right next to the dolphins.
